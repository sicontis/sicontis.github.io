Welcome to the Grey Lynn Returned Services Club

The Grey Lynn RSC is a great function venue, boasting a large dance floor upstairs with full bar and restaurant and meeting facilities for hire only. Perfect for a private function, party venue, wedding venue, business social or dine and dance.

We feature live bands every weekend, and host a whole bunch of events. We have our own TAB, 3 big screen Tv's with Sky TV, 3 pool tables, and 2 dartboards.

Established in 1937, the RSC became a social club for the local men and women who served in World War II. It's interior walls and decor are rich in history of our armed forces and their contributions with photos, plaques, and military items.

At 6pm every night, an ode is called and the lights are dimmed for 30 seconds silence to remember our fallen.
